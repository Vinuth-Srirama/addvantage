Dental Case Study:

The Goal

Fill appointment books, boost rebookings, and tailor oral health journeys for both new and prospective patients — ultimately increasing long-term value (LTV) of patients

The Problem

  • Appointment book sat 40–60% vacant, bleeding revenue.

  • Contract specialists frequently missed clinic sessions, leaving high-value chairs idle.

  • Existing marketing was ad hoc and disconnected from patient management systems.

  • Patients weren’t being systematically re-engaged for hygiene visits, treatment follow-ups, or recalls.

The Strategy

  • Installed a CRM integrated with practice management (D4W).

  • Automated marketing campaigns and funnels into the CRM.

  • Launched retargeting campaigns for unconverted leads

  • Automated client onboarding for both existing, and new patients

  • Added smart lead qualification to prioritize high-value procedures

  • Automated specialist scheduling workflows,

  • Developed dashboards to track patient LTV, util %, and ROI

  • Set up recurring revenue reactivation campaigns

Legal Case Study:

The Goal

Build a robust pipeline by optimising lead inquiries, re-engagement and automating document- heavy processes to reduce staff hours whilst staying compliant

The Problem

  • 84% of one-off leads from referral partners went cold after initial enquiry

  • SMS Outreach to conveyancing prospects was slow, ad-hoc, and occurred outside of business hours.

  • Manual doc prep consumed staff capacity, slowing turnaround and increased low-value “busywork.”

  • Off-the-shelf software offerings failed to meet Aus legal privacy standards.

The Strategy

  • Developed six custom-built AI agentic workflows for doc generation, doc reviewal, project management, invoice processing etc.

  • Integrated scheduling + SMS reminders for new client onboarding

  • Secure, industry-specific automation stack for Aus legal standards.

  • Installed a custom-built CRM for inbound leads and automated SMS and email campaigns in tone and voice of head conveyancing team.

Gym Case Study:

The Goal

Improve client retention, increase LTV as well as flagging high-risk members early via attendance & engagement data

The Problem

  • Trial sign-ups dropped off after promotional session, difficulty converting to high-tier membership.

  • Trainers had no structured way to spot disengagement early, relying on gut feel.

  • Churn was killing growth, with 7% attrition each month, forcing gym to replace its entire base annually.

  • Follow ups and promotional campaigns were not targeted and were not effective.

The Strategy

  • Automated attendance & engagement data tracking to proactively flag at-risk members for AI workflows for tailored plans and targeted promos.

  • Automated personalised re-engagement campaigns (SMS/email) for trialists, casuals and lapsed members.

  • Automated retargeting campaigns for warm and dormant leads, as well as lapsed and expired trial members.

  • Streamlined admin workflows (onboarding, reminders, billing, cash flow) from "busywork" to focus on coaching
